What is Shilajit?

Shilajit is a sticky, tar-like substance found primarily in the rocks of the Himalayas. It is formed over centuries by the slow decomposition of plant matter and is rich in fulvic acid, humic acid, and over 85 essential minerals. In Ayurveda, it is known as the "destroyer of weakness" and the "conqueror of mountains."
