Shipping Policy

Fast, reliable delivery across India. Know everything about how we ship your order.

Delivery Areas

We ship to all locations across India — metro cities, towns, and villages. No matter where you are, we ensure your order reaches you safely and on time.

Delivery Timeframe

Standard Delivery: 3–7 business days from the date of order confirmation. Delivery times may vary slightly based on your location and courier partner.

Orders are processed within 24 hours of placement. Once shipped, you will receive a tracking ID to monitor your package in real time.

Shipping Charges

We believe in keeping things simple and affordable:

  • Free Shipping: On all orders above ₹499.
  • Standard Shipping: ₹49 flat rate for orders below ₹499.
  • No hidden charges — what you see at checkout is what you pay.

Cash on Delivery (COD)

We offer Cash on Delivery across all serviceable pin codes in India. Pay only when your order arrives at your doorstep. No advance payment required. COD is available on all products with no extra charges.

Order Tracking

Once your order is dispatched, we will share a tracking number via WhatsApp or email. You can use this tracking number on our courier partner's website to track your package in real time.

Packaging

Your privacy is our priority. All orders are shipped in plain, discreet packaging with no indication of the contents or the brand name on the outside. Your order will appear as a standard courier package to ensure complete discretion.
